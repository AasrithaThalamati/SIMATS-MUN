Download comprehensive research materials for each committee

UNHRC

United Nations Human Rights Council

Explore critical human rights issues affecting populations worldwide. This committee addresses violations, promotes universal rights, and develops mechanisms for protection and accountability.

UNEP

United Nations Environment Programme

Tackle environmental challenges including climate change, biodiversity loss, and sustainable development. Develop innovative solutions for a greener future.

UNSC

United Nations Security Council

Engage in high-stakes discussions on international peace and security. Address conflicts, peacekeeping operations, and threats to global stability.

DISEC

Disarmament and International Security Committee

Debate disarmament issues, nuclear non-proliferation, and emerging security threats including cyber warfare and autonomous weapons systems.

WHO

World Health Organization

Address global health crises, pandemic preparedness, healthcare accessibility, and emerging diseases affecting populations worldwide.

UNODC

United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime

Combat transnational organized crime, drug trafficking, corruption, and terrorism through international cooperation and legal frameworks.
