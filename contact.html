Conference Venue

SIMATS Engineering, Saveetha University

Saveetha University
SIMATS Engineering Campus

Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

SIMATS MUN 2026 will be hosted at the distinguished campus of Saveetha University, SIMATS Engineering — a state-of-the-art academic institution equipped with modern conference infrastructure, structured committee rooms, high-speed connectivity, and professional auditorium facilities befitting an international-standard diplomatic simulation.

The campus offers a purpose-built environment for intellectual discourse, featuring spacious debate halls, collaborative lounges, and dedicated media spaces that collectively create an immersive conference experience.

Comprehensive venue instructions, committee room assignments, parking guidance, and reporting protocols will be shared with all registered delegates and faculty advisors in advance of the conference via official email communication.
