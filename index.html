About SIMATS MUN 2026

Diplomacy in Action

Welcome to the inaugural edition of SIMATS Model United Nations 2026, a prestigious platform where future diplomats converge to engage in intellectual discourse and diplomatic excellence. As we embark on this historic first edition, we invite bright minds from across the nation to participate in this transformative experience that simulates the grandeur and complexity of international relations.

01

Global Diplomacy

Our conference provides an immersive experience in global diplomacy, where delegates will debate pressing international issues, forge alliances, draft resolutions, and develop the leadership skills necessary to shape tomorrow's world through rigorous debate and collaborative problem-solving.

02

Academic Excellence

Participants will gain invaluable insights into the art of negotiation and the science of international cooperation, developing critical thinking, public speaking, and diplomatic skills that will serve them throughout their academic and professional careers.

03

First Edition Legacy

Be part of history as we launch the first-ever SIMATS MUN. This pioneering conference promises to set new standards in academic diplomacy, offering delegates an unparalleled opportunity to be the trailblazers of a legacy that will inspire generations to come.
