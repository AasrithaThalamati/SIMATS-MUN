SIMATS MUN Inaugural Edition · 2026
Welcome to the Assembly

Where future diplomats converge to engage in intellectual discourse and diplomatic excellence.

Welcome to the inaugural edition of SIMATS Model United Nations 2026, a prestigious platform hosted by Saveetha University, SIMATS Engineering. As we embark on this historic first edition, we invite bright minds from across the nation to participate in this transformative experience that simulates the grandeur and complexity of international relations.

SIMATS Model United Nations 2026 — Inaugural Edition

♦   ♦   ♦
Ref: SIMATS / MUN / 2026 / SG-001 25th April, 2026  ·  Chennai

To Every Distinguished Delegate,

It is with the deepest honour and profound pride that I extend to you, on behalf of the entire Secretariat of SIMATS Model United Nations 2026, this most heartfelt and official welcome. You stand at the threshold of an extraordinary chapter — one that will mark not only the inaugural edition of this conference, but a defining moment in your own journey as a future architect of global affairs.

“

The art of diplomacy is not merely the pursuit of agreement — it is the cultivation of understanding between peoples, the forging of bridges across the chasms of ideology and interest.

SIMATS MUN 2026 is conceived as a sanctuary of intellectual rigour and diplomatic excellence — a stage upon which the most pressing challenges of our epoch shall be examined with the gravity they deserve. Hosted under the distinguished patronage of Saveetha University, this conference unites over 150 delegates representing more than 50 nations across five distinguished committees, each dedicated to a domain of critical global consequence.

As you take your seat in the chambers of deliberation, I implore you to carry the weight of your portfolio with the full seriousness it commands. You are not merely a student bearing a placard; you are the sovereign voice of a nation, the steward of its people’s aspirations, and a participant in the grand tradition of multilateral diplomacy. The resolutions you draft, the alliances you forge, and the arguments you advance shall echo in these halls long after the gavel falls.

UNHRC ECOFIN AIPPM UNCSW IPC

The Secretariat has laboured with meticulous care to ensure that every dimension of this conference reflects the highest standards of academic diplomacy. We have designed an experience that challenges, inspires, and transforms. You will be called upon not merely to recite policy, but to defend principle; not merely to negotiate, but to lead.

I urge every delegate, every press corps journalist, every chair and member of the dais, to approach these proceedings with the spirit of Diplomacy in Action — our guiding motto and our abiding promise to you. Welcome to SIMATS MUN 2026. Welcome to history.

With distinguished regards and the highest diplomatic courtesies, Secretary-General SIMATS Model United Nations 2026 Saveetha University  ·  SIMATS Engineering
