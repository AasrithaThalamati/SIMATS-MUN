The Secretariat and Organising Committee are the bedrock upon which SIMATS MUN 2026 is built. Comprising dedicated student leaders, experienced faculty mentors, and passionate committee professionals, this team works collectively to ensure academic excellence, operational efficiency, procedural fairness, and an enriching, transformative experience for every delegate. United by a singular commitment to diplomatic education, they bring SIMATS MUN 2026 to life.
